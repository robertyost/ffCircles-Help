Overview

ffCircle is a private app for staying connected with the people who matter most. It gives your family a single place to keep track of contact information — phone numbers, email addresses, birthdays, anniversaries, and more — so that information is always handy and always up to date.

Beyond contacts, ffCircle lets family members share movie recommendations with each other. When someone watches something worth seeing, they can add a review and recommend it to the family. It's a simple way to keep a shared list of movies your family has watched and enjoyed.

Families

ffCircle is built around the idea of a family — a private group of people who share information with each other. You can belong to more than one family, which is useful if you want to stay connected with both your immediate family and a wider circle like extended family or close friends.

Each family is separate — members, contacts, and movie reviews are shared only within the family they belong to. For details on creating a family, joining an existing one, and managing members, see the Family help section.
