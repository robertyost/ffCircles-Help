Families

A family is a private group. Members of a family share contacts and movie reviews with each other. Nothing is visible outside the family.

Creating a Family

When you create a family you become its admin. You give the family a name and a family code is generated. You will need that code to invite others.

The Family Code

Each family has a unique code. This is how new members join — they enter the code on the Join Family screen. As admin you can share the code by SMS or copy it to the clipboard from the family screen.

Joining a Family

If someone has given you a family code, go to Join Family, enter the code, and you will be added as a member. You must have an account to join a family.

Multiple Families

You can belong to more than one family. Each family is independent — its members, contacts, and movie reviews are separate. When you are in a family's context you only see that family's data. You can switch between families from the main screen.

Members and Contacts

A family has two kinds of people. A member has an ffCircle account and can log in. A contact is someone added to the family's people list but does not have an account — they are there for reference only, such as a phone number or birthday you want to keep track of.

Admin

The admin can invite members, promote or demote other members to admin, and manage the family. There can be more than one admin in a family.
