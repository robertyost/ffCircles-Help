The Edit Details screen allows you to update information for a family member or contact. Make your changes and tap Save when done — either the save icon in the top right or the save button at the bottom of the screen.

Email

Email is a special field and cannot be changed by simply editing it. Enter the new email address in the Update Email Address field and tap the Change button next to it. This is handled separately to protect the account login for members who use their email to sign in.

Admin Only

If you are an admin you will see additional options at the bottom of the screen that are not visible to regular members:

  • Role — promote or demote a member to or from admin.
  • Active / Inactive — deactivate a member to remove their access to the family without deleting their record. This only affects their status in your family and has no effect on any other family they belong to.
