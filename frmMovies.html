Movies

The Movies section lets your family keep a shared list of films you want to watch or have watched, with ratings and reviews from each member.

The Family Movie List

The Family Movies screen shows all movies that have been added to your family's list. Each movie is displayed as a poster with its title and release year. Tap any movie to open its detail and review screen.

Use the Search bar at the top to filter the list by title as you type. The Recommended By dropdown lets you narrow the list to movies recommended by a specific family member. The Select Genre dropdown filters by genre. Use them together to quickly find what you're looking for.

Adding a Movie

Tap the + button in the bottom right corner of the Family Movies screen to search for a movie to add. Type part of the movie title and a list of matches will appear. Tap a result to see its details — poster, year, runtime, genres, and a description pulled from the movie database.

Below the movie details you will see a Recommendations for you section showing similar films. Tap any of them to explore instead, and keep browsing until you find what you want to add.

When you're ready to add the movie, tap the + button next to the movie details. If you belong to more than one family, you will be asked which families to share it with — all your families are checked by default, so uncheck any you want to exclude, then tap OK.

If a movie is already in a family's list, that will be indicated next to the + button for that family.

You can optionally check Add Recommendation at this point as a quick way to mark that you recommend the movie without writing a full review.

Movie Reviews

Tap any movie on the Family Movies screen to open the Movie Reviews screen. At the top you will see the movie's poster, title, year, runtime, genres, and a synopsis. The Do I recommend checkbox shows whether you have marked this movie as recommended.

Below that, any reviews written by family members are listed, each showing the member's name, the date, their star rating (1–5), and their written review if they added one.

To add your review, tap the pencil icon in the bottom right corner. You can set three things:

  • Recommend — a simple yes/no on whether you recommend the film
  • Star rating — 1 to 5 stars
  • Review — optional written comments

You can have only one review per movie. The same pencil button lets you come back and edit it at any time. To delete your review, tap the trash icon next to your review entry.
